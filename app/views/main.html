About Philos

Philos.io is built behind an unique concept: Training and mentoring people on a technology by building together an application in One Week.

The web and internet are our playgrounds and we want to help you take part to the game as well! So by joining our blue ship trainings you will be able to build amazing things. From one week to another you will be able to change you career or simply change the way you approach your daily tasks!

From building mobile application in one week to designing advanced backend API, at our Hacker Dojo you will empower yourselft with new skills.

Does your company need a blue ship training on the latest web technologies? We have the solution with our corporate trainings! You can either send your entire team at our Hacker Dojo or we can send our mentors on-site in your company.
